# GRAAVITONS SMS - Feature Overview

Prepared for client presentation. This document consolidates all product features available in the application.

## 1) Access and Security
- Role-based login (Admin/Teacher)
- Protected API access and authenticated sessions
- Controlled access to operational and analytics modules

## 2) Batch Lifecycle Management
- Create, list, rename, and manage academic batches
- Organize batches by academic year, program type, and subject configuration
- Batch-level controls for student and exam workflows

## 3) Student Information Management
- Unified student profile with academic and personal details
- Parent/guardian information management
- 10th and 12th marks tracking
- Entrance exam details (name, percentile, mark)
- Counselling details with multi-forum entries
- Student-level update and maintenance workflows

## 4) Student Onboarding at Scale
- Individual student creation
- Bulk student upload via Excel templates
- Bulk update/edit support through structured sheets

## 5) Exam and Marks Management
- Unit test and monthly test entry workflows
- Batch-based and date-based exam organization
- Support for numeric and non-numeric mark entries (example: absence)
- Subject-wise and total marks capture
- Excel-based upload for unit and monthly tests
- Automated total calculation for monthly tests

## 6) Analytics and Performance Intelligence
- Subject-wise analysis with filters (batch, date range, subject)
- Branch-wise analysis to compare learning groups
- Individual student analysis with trends and feedback
- Batch performance dashboard with trends, distributions, and participation
- Advanced batch analytics: median, quartiles, percentiles, outlier signals
- Risk scoring using participation and performance trends
- Weak-topic diagnostics for targeted remediation
- Subject diagnostics at batch level (weak/improving segments)

## 7) Feedback and Intervention Tools
- Per-student feedback notes
- Feedback history integrated into student analysis
- Early-warning indicators and intervention support via analytics

## 8) Achiever Showcase
- Add, view, edit, and delete achiever records
- Dashboard-ready achievement presentation

## 9) Reporting, Exports, and Templates
- Batch performance report generation
- Excel template download for unit and monthly tests
- Template-driven Excel uploads with validations
- Structured exports for reporting and audits
- PDF and Excel export support

## 10) Operational Efficiency Features
- Standardized templates to reduce manual errors
- Bulk imports and repeatable workflows
- Consistent reporting for management and stakeholder communication
